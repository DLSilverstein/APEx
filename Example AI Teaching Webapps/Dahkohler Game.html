Master the Damköhler Number

The Damköhler number ($Da$) is a dimensionless group that relates the rate of reaction to the rate of transport (convection) in a reactor.

Your task is to correctly derive $Da$ and the final performance equation ($X$ vs $Da$) for four different scenarios covering:

  • Reactions: $A + B \rightarrow C$ and $A \rightleftharpoons B$
  • Reactors: CSTR and PFR

Note: We are focusing strictly on reaction engineering (isothermal, homogeneous). Energy and mass transfer Damköhler numbers are excluded.
